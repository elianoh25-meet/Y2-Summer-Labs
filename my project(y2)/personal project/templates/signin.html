The That Girl app will help you work on time management,
self-acceptance, and self-love by allowing you to try out a day
in the life of someone from a curated list.
This will help you learn new time management techniques, understand your strengths,
and improve your self-esteem, ultimately making you a happier,
calmer, and better person.
You will be able to go on your own journey to happiness
and summarize the things you take away from that day to see your own personal
growth and progress.So come on this journey, just for you.
